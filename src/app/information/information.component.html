Player Implementations

There are multiple implementations to compete with each others

Player - Human

The Player Human is YOU! Just click on the board to play.

Robot - Random

The dumbest player! It just randomly choose a free cell.

Robot - Minimax

The 'perfect' player! But also the slowest. It tries out all possible combinations to decide the best move.

The project has been done by TEIT Batch D Issac Samuel , Alec Derose , Sayali Pawar , Aditya Shrotriya

In additional to the simple rules to categories WIN, DRAW and LOSS, this minimax implementation has two improvements:

  • Uses the depth in the score to prefer winning faster and losing slower.
  • Small adjustment of the score to prefer DRAWs with higher possibilities to win and lower possibilities to lose
